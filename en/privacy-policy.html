Analytics & Behavioral Tracking (The "Why")
To improve our portfolio experience and understand how visitors interact with our work, we use the following tools only after your explicit consent: Google Analytics 4 (GA4): Collects anonymized traffic data (country, device, pages visited). IP addresses are anonymized; Microsoft Clarity: We use this to analyze user behavior through heatmaps and session replays. It helps us see where users linger and what content is most engaging. Note: Clarity anonymizes data and does not record sensitive input or keystrokes.

Legal Basis for Processing

Data Retention & Third-Party Transfers

Your Rights
You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Since we do not store names or personal IDs, the most effective way to exercise these rights is to clear your browser's LocalStorage and Cookies.
