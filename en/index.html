About

Anna Kryzhanivska: Post Production Agency

We are a founder led agency specializing in high volume ecommerce and high end fashion image editing. We operate remotely as an outsource photo retouching partner for brands and photographers globally including major fashion capitals and financial hubs.

The Trajectory

The agency evolved from Anna's professional background in photography. Transitioning into specialized retouching, she achieved a placement in the top 0.5% of talent on a leading UK based freelance platform within eighteen months. In 2019, this performance was recognized with the Female Freelancer of the Year award.

A Decade of Scale

Ten years of industry operations have defined our expertise in managing high capacity workflows while maintaining visual integrity. The team provides scalable photo post production for fashion campaigns and large scale ecommerce catalogs, meeting the aesthetic and technical standards of premium international markets.

Technical Expertise

Our technical approach balances production efficiency with technical precision. We optimize workflows to deliver consistent results and rational resource management. The team handles garment symmetry, defect removal, and model skin work to ensure multi SKU consistency across large volumes of assets. Core capabilities focus on image retouching for fashion and ecommerce catalogs.

The Team

We are powered by a team of skilled Ukrainian women retouchers. This structure provides the technical mastery and operational bandwidth required for high volume editing and specific retouching protocols. We function as an extension of client production teams, adhering to strict quality assurance and color critical accuracy.

Market Alignment

Our infrastructure meets global creative market standards and provides visual continuity for brands across international regions. We remain flexible and open to the specific requirements and individual preferences of every client. Full discretion and non disclosure agreements are available upon request to ensure project security.
