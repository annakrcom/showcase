About

We're a remote retouching agency specializing in high-volume e-commerce and fashion images, based in Europe and operating worldwide. Our fully female team is made up of experienced and young passionate professionals.

We stay consistent, and we operate with any level of required discretion — NDA, no micromanagement, no before vs after published. Mostly experienced with fashion, beauty, sport and jewellery catalogs and campaigns.

Most of our clients are long-term partners who trust us to handle post-production quietly and seamlessly behind the scenes while saving up to 90% of managerial time resources and spending up to 70% less of the budgets.

My own journey began as a teenage photographer, camera in hand, chasing stories through images. Over time, I fell in love with post-production — the magic that happens after the shutter clicks.

What started as solo freelancing turned into a decade of fruitful collaboration with e-commerce and fashion brands. I became known as a top-level professional, and in 2019, I was honored to receive the Female Freelancer of the Year award in the UK.

Eventually, along the way, there was even more to offer — not just through my own hands, but by bringing together a trusted team of women who shared my values: precision, care, and discretion. Combining my business education and experience in photography, that's how this agency was born.

It's more than a business — it's the natural evolution of a passion I've carried for years.
