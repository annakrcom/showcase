Questions

Frequently asked

What's your typical turnaround time?

Most projects are delivered within 3–7 business days, depending on volume and complexity. Rush options are available.

Which file formats do you accept?

I work with RAW, TIFF, and high-quality JPEG files. PSDs with layers are welcome for advanced compositing work.

How many revisions are included?

Each image includes two rounds of revisions. Additional rounds are billed at a flat rate per image.

How do you price your work?

Pricing depends on the type of retouching, image volume, and deadline. Get in touch for a custom quote.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes — I'm happy to sign NDAs and treat all client material as confidential by default.
